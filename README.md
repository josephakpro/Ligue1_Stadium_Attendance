## Project Description

This repository contains a comprehensive analysis evaluating league-wide attendance dynamics, stadium efficiency, and fan engagement across French football during the 2025/2026 season. By synthesizing macro trends, regional demographics, and granular club-level behavior from seasonal tracking dashboards, this project explores the drivers of stadium occupancy.

Evaluating the Occupancy Rate goes beyond raw volume to highlight the reality of local market penetration, seasonal demand shifts, and fan loyalty.

## Key Insights

•	League Benchmark: Total attendance reached 8.39M fans with a strong league-wide average occupancy rate of 82.32%.

•	Prestige Drivers: Major clubs like Paris SG and Marseille always create a massive demand surge when visiting, driving an average occupancy of 91.16% (+9.95% lift over baseline).

•	Entertainment Value: High-scoring matches secured an average occupancy of 86.23% (+5.05% lift over baseline), proving that fluid attacking football directly impacts club revenue.

•	Top Performer: Lens anchored the league's efficiency, pushing nearly 100% capacity utilization every single matchday.

•	Lowest Performer: Monaco struggled with severe under-utilization, averaging just 44% occupancy.

## Data Story & Regional Trends

•	Macro-Level Trends & Seasonal Volatility: Tracking the seasonal timeline revealed a sharp Winter Slump dropping to a 75% occupancy floor in late January 2026, followed by a Spring Resurgence peaking at 92% in April 2026 as high-stakes title races heated up.

•	Regional Powerhouses vs. Local Market Failures: A sharp contrast was observed between the Southern Engine (Marseille's Orange Vélodrome boasting 93% occupancy on a 67,394 stadium capacity) and the Côte d'Azur Paradox (Nice at 61% occupancy and Monaco at a league-worst 44% occupancy).

•	Club Loyalty Profiles: Fan behavior was categorized into distinct profiles, contrasting the "Die Hard" (Brest, Lens...) with low variance, against the "Glory Hunters Clubs" (Nantes, Nice...) in this case, which rely heavily on high-profile opponents to fill the stadium.
